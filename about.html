About

Mutex Markets is a major liquidity provider in the FX, CFD and futures markets. Partnering with brokers, exchanges, ECNs and technology providers across Europe, Americas, Asia and Australia we have a truly global presence. We offer solutions ranging from reliable liquidity to risk management on a per customer basis meeting your requirements where you currently are with a key focus on cost effectiveness.

Our global presence coupled with alpha driven models and risk management allow us to offer some of the most competitive liquidity at lowest costs, regardless of changing market conditions.

The Team

Given the highly competitive nature of the financial markets over the last 10 years, our core value as a key ingredient to success is people. A combination of the brightest minds, ambition and experience allows us to operate with a small team, developing cutting edge technology and trading models. All of our technology and trading team are educated to PhD or master level from top UK Universities including Imperial College London, University of Cambridge and Warwick University.
